About Lab:






















Our lab is focused on the research of insect biochemical and molecular biology. The work mainly focuses on the studies of insect immune chemistry, many progresses have been made revealing the serine protease cascade reaction system in insect blood and its multiple regulation methods and clarifying the framework of the extracellular immune signal recognition transduction regulation system. The research fields in this lab cover insect comparative genomics, transcription and proteomics, recombinant protein expression, purification and functional analysis, enzyme and inhibitor kinetics research, protein localization mutagenesis and three-dimensional structure analysis.

We are looking for self-motivated students who are interested in pursuing Ph.D. degrees on the topic of insect biochemical and molecular biology.
